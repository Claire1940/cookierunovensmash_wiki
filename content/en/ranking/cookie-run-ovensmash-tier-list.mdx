## S-Tier: The Meta-Definers

The cookies in this tier are currently considered "must-haves" for any competitive roster. They possess abilities that can turn the tide of a match single-handedly.

### Red Velvet Cookie (Bruiser)
Red Velvet is widely considered the most "broken" character in the current **cookie run ovensmash tier list**. His special ability allows him to shoot out a hand that grabs enemies, pulls them in, and stuns them. More importantly, he gains **Vampirism** for five seconds, allowing him to steal HP and sustain himself in the middle of a brawl. His ultimate, which summons Cake Hounds after a massive slam, provides both high damage and distraction.

### Peach Cookie (Assassin)
Peach Cookie is the bane of Marksmen. Her basic attack involves a leaping strike that makes her incredibly difficult to hit with skill shots. Her ultimate is a "spin to win" mechanic where she becomes **unstoppable**, meaning she cannot be interrupted by stuns or freezes while dealing continuous damage for three seconds.

### Jerky Cookie (Mage)
Jerky Cookie excels at punishing grouped-up teams. Her ultimate fires a talisman that attaches to an enemy; if that enemy touches a teammate, the explosion transfers. This can lead to instant team wipes if the opposing team doesn't scatter immediately.

## A-Tier: Strong and Reliable

These cookies are highly effective and can compete with S-tier characters when played with high skill. They often provide essential utility like tanking or long-range pressure.

### Blue Slushy Cookie (Marksman)
Blue Slushy is the premier choice for players who struggle with environmental obstacles. Her ultimate summons five clones that fire piercing arrows. These arrows travel through walls and map objectives (like the turtle in Castle Crasher), making it impossible for enemies to hide.

### Madeleine Cookie (Tank)
As an Epic tank, Madeleine is vital for initiating fights. His ultimate allows him to leap to a designated area while **invincible**, ensuring his crowd control (knocking enemies airborne) cannot be stopped. He is the gold standard for frontline durability.

### Peppermint Cookie (Support)
Despite being a Common rarity, Peppermint is arguably the best healer in the game. Their special ability restores **424 HP per second** to nearby allies for five seconds. When combined with an ultimate that summons a giant whale to knock enemies up, Peppermint provides a perfect balance of sustain and disruption.

## B and C Tiers: Niche and Underperforming

While every cookie can be fun to play, those in the lower tiers of the **cookie run ovensmash tier list** generally lack the impact needed for high-level play in 2026.

*   **Latte Cookie (B):** Her damage output is currently lower than other mages like Blueberry Pie. While her invulnerability during her staff ride is a great escape, she lacks the "kill pressure" required for the current meta.
*   **Strawberry Crepe (B):** A decent tank that reduces enemy attack power, but often overshadowed by Madeleine's invincibility frames.
*   **GingerBrave (C):** Unfortunately, the face of the franchise struggles in OvenSmash. His only real utility is his ultimate, which provides a speed trail to help allies return to the fight faster after respawning.
*   **Tiger Lily (C):** Her kit is very basic. Without strong crowd control or piercing damage, she is easily outclassed by Blue Slushy or even Rye Cookie.

## Role-Based Strategy Guide

To build a winning team, you must balance your composition. Simply picking the top four cookies from the **cookie run ovensmash tier list** won't work if you lack a frontline or a healer.

### The Ideal Team Comp
1.  **The Anchor (Tank):** Madeleine or Strawberry Crepe to soak up damage and initiate.
2.  **The Finisher (Assassin/Bruiser):** Red Velvet or Peach to dive the backline.
3.  **The Artillery (Mage/Marksman):** Blue Slushy or Jerky for area-of-effect pressure.
4.  **The Lifeblood (Support):** Peppermint or Melon Soda to maintain the team's HP.

> **Warning:** Avoid teams with three or more Marksmen. While the damage is high, you will be easily wiped by a single Peach Cookie or Red Velvet dive.
