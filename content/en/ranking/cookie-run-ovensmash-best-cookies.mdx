**Q: Who is the absolute best cookie for beginners?**

A: Generally, no. While GingerBrave is the face of the franchise, he is currently ranked in the C-tier. His only real utility is using his Ultimate to speed back into the battle after respawning. Most other Bruisers offer much more combat value.

**Q: How do I beat a Peach Cookie player?**

A: To counter Peach Cookie, you need "Unstoppable" or "Immune" effects. Fruit Punch Cookie’s giant form makes him immune to crowd control, which can help him survive her spin. Alternatively, use a high-burst Mage like Blueberry Pie to take her down before she can start her rotation.

**Q: What makes a cookie "S-Tier" in OvenSmash?**

A: The **cookie run ovensmash best cookies** in S-Tier usually possess a combination of high damage, crowd control (stuns/freezes), and some form of "Unstoppable" or "Invincible" frames that prevent their abilities from being canceled by the enemy.
