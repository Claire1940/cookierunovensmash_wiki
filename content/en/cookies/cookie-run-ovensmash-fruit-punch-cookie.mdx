## Who is Fruit Punch Cookie?

Fruit Punch Cookie is not your average snack. In the world of *Cookie Run: OvenSmash*, he is classified as a Tank, meaning his primary responsibility is to soak up damage and disrupt the enemy team's positioning.

He is famously the proprietor of the Café Fruit Bomber, a location well-known to fans of the franchise. ## Advanced Strategies for 2026

To truly master the **cookie run ovensmash fruit punch cookie**, you need to think beyond just pressing buttons. Positioning is the most critical factor for a Tank in the current [Cookie Run: OvenSmash](https://www.cookierun-ovensmash.com/en) environment.

### 1. The "Bodyguard" Playstyle
Because his ultimate provides a 50% damage reduction shield, you should always stay near your most vulnerable allies. If you see a Blue Slushy Cookie or a Peppermint Cookie being targeted by an assassin, use your Power Tackle to peel the enemy away and immediately drop your "Hit Me If You Dare!" barrier.

### 2. Initiating with the Tackle
Don't wait for the enemy to come to you. The 11.0 range on the Power Tackle (Power version) allows you to initiate fights from a distance. If you can catch two or more enemies grouped together, you can push them into a corner, making it easy for your teammates to follow up with Area of Effect (AoE) attacks.

### 3. Managing the Ultimate's Size Increase
When using the Power version of his ultimate, Fruit Punch Cookie grows significantly larger. While the CC immunity is a massive advantage, remember that you will soak up almost every projectile fired in your direction. Use this time to block paths or "body block" for your team, but be careful not to overextend if your healers are down.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not activate your ultimate if you are at low health and away from your team. The increased size makes you a "bullet magnet," and without support, you may be eliminated before the 10-second buff expires.
