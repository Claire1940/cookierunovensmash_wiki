## Exploring the Game Modes of OvenSmash

CookieRun: OvenSmash is not a traditional runner; it is a multi-faceted combat game featuring six distinct modes at launch. Each mode requires a different strategy, ranging from team-based objective control to classic battle royale survival.

### Castle Crasher (Two-Team Battle)
This is a strategic escort mode. Your team must capture a giant Turtle and protect it as it marches toward the enemy castle. The goal is to escort the Turtle to the designated location or destroy the enemy's fortifications entirely.

### Drop the Beat (Two-Team Battle)
Focusing on area control, teams must capture a central stage in the middle of the map. Staying on the stage earns points over time. The first team to reach the target score wins the match.

### Wild Royale (Free-for-All)
The classic survival experience. Players are dropped into a shrinking arena where only one Cookie can remain standing. Scavenge for power-ups and avoid environmental hazards to be the last survivor.

### Mode Comparison Table
