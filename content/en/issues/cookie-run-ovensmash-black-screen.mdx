## Platform-Specific Solutions for 2026

The way Android and iOS handle game data differs significantly. Depending on your device, you may need to perform a "Cache Clear" or an "App Offload" to fix the **cookie run ovensmash black screen**.

### Fixes for iOS Users (iPhone & iPad)
Apple devices use a sandboxed system where you cannot manually delete cache folders easily. Instead, you should use the "Offload" feature. This removes the app's binary files (which might be corrupted) but keeps your local save data intact.

1.  Open **Settings** and navigate to **General**.
2.  Select **iPhone Storage** (or iPad Storage).
3.  Locate **Cookie Run: OvenSmash** in the list.
4.  Tap **Offload App** and confirm.
5.  Once finished, tap **Reinstall App**.

### Fixes for Android Users
Android provides more granular control over app data. If you are stuck on a **cookie run ovensmash black screen**, clearing the cache is your best bet before attempting a full reinstallation.

| Step | Action | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Step 1** | Go to Settings > Apps > OvenSmash. | Access app management. |
| **Step 2** | Select **Storage & Cache**. | View data usage. |
| **Step 3** | Tap **Clear Cache**. | Removes temporary files. |
| **Step 4** | Tap **Force Stop**. | Ends all active processes. |
| **Step 5** | Relaunch the game. | Test the fix. |

> **💡 Tip:** If clearing the cache doesn't work, you can try "Clear Data," but ensure your account is linked to Google Play or DevPlay first, as this will delete your login info.
