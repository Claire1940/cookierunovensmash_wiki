Since OvenSmash is a real-time multiplayer game, your internet connection is the most vital component. Even a "fast" connection can suffer from packet loss, leading to a "Not Working" state.

| Connection Method | Potential Problem | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|

## Advanced Troubleshooting for Persistent Crashes

If basic steps fail, you may need to look deeper into your device's settings. 

### Offloading and Reinstalling (iOS)
iOS offers a unique feature called "Offloading." This removes the app's binary files while keeping your local save data intact. Go to **Settings > General > iPhone Storage > CookieRun: OvenSmash** and tap **Offload App**. Afterward, tap **Reinstall App**. This often fixes "Black Screen" issues without requiring a full 4GB+ download of all game assets.

### System Software Updates
Modern games are built on the latest APIs (Application Programming Interfaces). If your phone is running an older version of iOS or Android, the game engine may encounter fatal errors. Ensure your device is updated to the latest OS version by checking your system settings.

### Storage Space Management
CookieRun: OvenSmash requires at least 5GB of free space for the initial install and additional "buffer" space for unpacking update files. If your storage is 95% full, the game may crash during the loading sequence because it cannot write temporary files to the disk.
