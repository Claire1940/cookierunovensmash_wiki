## How to Farm Rainbow Cubes and Crystals

To unlock the best **cookie run ovensmash exclusive costumes**, you will need a steady supply of Rainbow Cubes. While direct purchase is an option, F2P (Free to Play) players can utilize several in-game systems to build their collection.

1.  **Daily Smash Missions**: Completing three matches per day yields a small bounty of Rainbow Cubes.
2.  **Guild Raids**: Participating in weekly guild bosses provides "Guild Coins," which can be exchanged for Costume Gacha Tickets in the Guild Shop.
3.  **Event Milestones**: Most seasonal events (like the 2026 Spring Blossom Festival) offer an Epic-tier costume as the final reward for completing the event track.
4.  **Costume Mileage**: Every duplicate costume pulled from the Gacha grants Rainbow Crystals. These are the only way to purchase Super Epic skins directly from the shop.

**Q: Can I transfer my costumes from Cookie Run: OvenBreak to OvenSmash?**

**Q: Do cookie run ovensmash exclusive costumes expire?**

A: Once you unlock a costume, it is yours forever. However, the *opportunity* to buy certain exclusive skins is often limited to specific event windows in 2026.

**Q: What is the best way to get Rainbow Crystals?**

A: Rainbow Crystals are primarily earned by pulling duplicate costumes in the Gacha. If you are hunting for a specific Super Epic skin, focusing your pulls on a banner where you already own most of the Common and Rare skins is the fastest way to accumulate crystals.

**Q: Are there any hidden stats on Legendary costumes?**

A: No. Devsisters has confirmed that all **cookie run ovensmash exclusive costumes** are purely cosmetic to ensure a fair competitive environment in the arena. The "buffs" mentioned in some menus refer to cosmetic score bonuses used in non-combat modes.
