## Map Objectives and Team Tactics

The core gameplay revolves around the Red Team vs. Blue Team dynamic. Each team has a castle that must be defended while simultaneously attacking the opposing side's fortifications.

### The Push Mechanic
As you defeat enemies and clear obstacles, your "Push" meter increases. A strong push allows your team to deal massive damage to the enemy's castle. When the announcer shouts that the "Red team's castle is down to half," it is a signal to either go all-in on the attack or fall back for a desperate defense.

### Overtime and Sudden Death
If the match is not decided within the standard time limit, the game enters "Overtime." During this phase, respawn timers are increased, and the power of all Cookies is scaled up. This is the most common time to see "Penta Kills" as the map becomes smaller and the stakes become higher.

> **Warning:** During Overtime, losing even one teammate can lead to an immediate loss. Stick together and focus your fire on a single target to secure the advantage.
