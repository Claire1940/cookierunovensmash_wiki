Combat in OvenSmash is fast and vertical. Each Cookie comes equipped with a unique set of skills, ranging from Rye Cookie's precision marksmanship to Latte Cookie's area-of-effect "Latte Magic." Mastering the timing of these abilities is crucial for securing multi-kills.
