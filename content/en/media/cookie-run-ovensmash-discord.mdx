## Navigating the 2026 Game Modes

CookieRun: OvenSmash offers a diverse array of modes that require different strategies. Discussing these in the **cookie run ovensmash discord** allows you to build specialized teams for each objective. According to the [official Devsisters site](https://game.devsisters.com/ovensmash/), the game currently features six primary rotations.

### Mode Breakdown and Strategy

1.  **Castle Crasher**: A two-team battle where you must capture a Turtle and escort it to the enemy castle. Coordination is key here; you need a tank to lead the Turtle and supports to keep the "pusher" alive.
2.  **Gnome Battle**: Teams collect gnomes across the map to build structures. This mode is highly mobile, favoring Cookies with high movement speed or dash abilities.
3.  **Wild Royale**: The classic free-for-all. Survival is the only goal. Discord members often share the best "bush-camping" or "aggressive-hunting" spots for specific maps.
4.  **Drop the Beat**: A king-of-the-hill style mode where you capture a central stage. Area-of-effect (AoE) Cookies like Latte or Chili Pepper excel here.
5.  **Jelly Race**: A fast-paced objective mode where you deposit Jellies into the enemy goal. 
6.  **Smash Fight**: Pure Team Deathmatch. The first team to reach the elimination cap wins.
